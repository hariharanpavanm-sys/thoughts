## 📖 Welcome to my Journal
This blog is a compilation of reflections and writings, created on SwamiG's suggestion, to share thoughts on life, philosophy, and Vedanta.

---

## 🔑 How to Access the Journal
To access and read the journal entries, you do **not** need a password:
1. Simply visit the website link.
2. Enter your **Name** (Username) in the login field.
3. Leave the **Password** field blank.
4. Click **Enter** to unlock and access the journal!

*(The password field is reserved only for Admin access).*

---

## 💬 How to Interact
Once unlocked, you can read and engage with the notes:
* **Search & Filters:** Use the search bar or tags to find specific reflections.
* **Likes & Views:** Click the heart icon to like thoughts that resonate with you.
* **Reflections (Comments):** Add your own reflections and thoughts on any post.
* **Feedback:** Share your rating and general feedback about the journal.
